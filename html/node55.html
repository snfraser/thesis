Collated from image archive via FITS headers, processed image count Fig. 5.6.1 - details of extraction - removal of outliers - these are caused by various sources.

  • Some images are deliberately defocussed or telescope out of focus.
  • Images of extended sources cause problems for reduction pipeline.
  • Other general pipeline problems.
